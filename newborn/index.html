    How Early Should I Schedule an Appointment?

    Technically, I require 3 weeks advance notice to schedule a session, however I book up quickly, so would encourage you to reach out as soon as possible so I can put your due-date on the books.

    How Old Should my Infant Be?

    The best time to book a session is when your newborn is between 0 and 10 days old. During these first days home from the hospital your baby will be spending most of his or her time sleeping, perfect for a photo-session!

    How many landing pages can I create?

    You may create as many pages as you like per single project. This means that if you plan to use Launchkit for another project, you will need to purchase an additional standard license.

    Do You Offer Print Products?

    Yes! I work with one of the best printers in the industry, to ensure that your print products come out incredible, and you are 100% satisfied.

    What Should I Wear to My Session?

    My style is very natural and organic; I tend to use neutral or pastel colors in my photos. In order to keep the photos harmonious, I recommend wearing solid clothing in a neutral color palette. Try to avoid bold prints, bright colors or any clothing with words on it. Parents and siblings should coordinate but not match perfectly. We want to create a beautiful, complimentary and timeless piece of art for you to enjoy for years.
